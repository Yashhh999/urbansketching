My Messy Sketch Collection

Here's all the random stuff I've drawn around town - some turned out pretty decent, others are total disasters but I'm posting them anyway

This gallery is basically my visual diary of wandering around the city. I've sketched everything from fancy buildings to weird street performers - honestly just whatever catches my eye that day.

My setup is super basic - usually just a crappy pen I bought at CVS, this tiny watercolor set my aunt got me for Christmas, and some colored pencils when I remember to bring them. It's really not about having expensive stuff.

Looking back at my old drawings is hilarious - my people used to look like stick figures that got run over by a truck! Now they actually look somewhat human, which is progress I guess. Plus I've discovered so many cool spots in my neighborhood that I never knew existed.
